Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Welcome to Motify! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Motify ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application, Motify (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

A Clear Note on Proximity Data

Our service connects you with nearby users by using your device's Bluetooth signal. We want to be clear:

  • We do not use or request GPS location data. Our functionality is based on short-range Bluetooth proximity only.
  • To be compliant with app store policies, we declare the collection of "Approximate Location." This is because using Bluetooth to detect nearby devices is categorized as a form of approximate location by platform operators. Our use is strictly for discovering other users in your immediate vicinity, not for tracking your physical location history.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: To create and secure your account, we collect your phone number for verification.
  • Profile Content: You may voluntarily add information to build your profile, including your name, biography, photos, and any other information you choose to share.

b) Information We Collect Automatically

  • Proximity & Connection Data: We use Bluetooth signals to detect nearby users. When a connection is made, we store an encrypted record linking the user identifiers, time, and connection status.
  • Usage Information: We log activity on our services, including usage dates and times, features used, and how you interact with other users.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, and Bluetooth settings.
  • Diagnostic & Log Data: We automatically collect log files and crash reports to help diagnose and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account and profile.
  • Enable the core proximity-based discovery and connection features.
  • Display your profile to other nearby users.
  • Ensure the security of our platform and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Perform diagnostics, fix bugs, and improve the App's functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Other Users: Your public profile is visible to other users when you are nearby.
  • With Our Service Providers: We use third-party vendors (primarily Google Firebase) to help operate, secure, and analyze our App. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • For Corporate Transactions: We may transfer your information in connection with a merger, sale, acquisition, or restructuring.
  • When Required by Law: We may disclose your information to comply with legal process, assist in crime prevention, or protect the safety of any person.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your personal information only as long as needed for legitimate business purposes. When you delete your account, we begin removing your data from our servers immediately. Residual data may remain in secure backups for a limited time before being permanently erased.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access / Portability: You can request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update your information through your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Delete your account at any time directly through the App.
  • Permissions: Manage app permissions (Bluetooth, Notifications) in your device settings.

6. Security

We use technical and administrative security measures, including encrypting data in transit and at rest. The identifier broadcasted via Bluetooth is encrypted to protect your identity.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers maintain facilities. These countries may have different data protection laws than your own.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are restricted to users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not permit users under the age of 18 on our platform.

9. Child Safety Standards

Motify has a zero-tolerance policy towards Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation. We enforce a strict age gate during registration, prohibit CSAE content in our terms of service, provide in-app reporting tools, and will report any discovered instances to relevant authorities including NCMEC.

10. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open the Motify app.
  2. Navigate to the Profile screen.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm that you wish to permanently delete your account.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
