Support

Find answers to common questions or get in touch.

How does Motify work?

Motify uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to detect other Motify users near you. When you're in proximity, you'll both appear on each other's radar. If you both like each other, it's a match and you can start chatting.

Does Motify track my location?

No. Motify does not use GPS or track your location. We only use Bluetooth to detect when other users are physically nearby. We never know or store where you are.

Why does Motify need Bluetooth permission?

Bluetooth is the core technology that powers Motify. It's how we detect nearby users. Without Bluetooth permission, the app cannot function.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Go to your Profile screen
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion

All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

How do I report a user?

Open the user's profile and tap the Report button. All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action will be taken.

The app isn't finding anyone nearby

Make sure Bluetooth is turned on and that Motify has Bluetooth permission in your device settings. The app needs to be running (even in the background) to detect nearby users.

I'm not receiving notifications

Check that notifications are enabled for Motify in your device settings. On Android, also make sure battery optimization isn't restricting the app.

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Motify! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Motify ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application, Motify (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

A Clear Note on Proximity Data

Our service connects you with nearby users by using your device's Bluetooth signal. We want to be clear:

  • We do not use or request GPS location data. Our functionality is based on short-range Bluetooth proximity only.
  • To be compliant with app store policies, we declare the collection of "Approximate Location." This is because using Bluetooth to detect nearby devices is categorized as a form of approximate location by platform operators. Our use is strictly for discovering other users in your immediate vicinity, not for tracking your physical location history.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: To create and secure your account, we collect your phone number for verification.
  • Profile Content: You may voluntarily add information to build your profile, including your name, biography, photos, and any other information you choose to share.

b) Information We Collect Automatically

  • Proximity & Connection Data: We use Bluetooth signals to detect nearby users. When a connection is made, we store an encrypted record linking the user identifiers, time, and connection status.
  • Usage Information: We log activity on our services, including usage dates and times, features used, and how you interact with other users.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, and Bluetooth settings.
  • Diagnostic & Log Data: We automatically collect log files and crash reports to help diagnose and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account and profile.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Other Users: Your public profile is visible to other users when you are nearby.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your personal information only as long as needed for legitimate business purposes. When you delete your account, we begin removing your data from our servers immediately. Residual data may remain in secure backups for a limited time before being permanently erased.
